Staff Cost Scenario Planner
Business Case — Internal Tool Deployment  ·  FY 2025–26
Prepared by: Sr. Director, People & Culture
Date: May 2026
Status: For Review & Approval
Classification: Internal

NI operates across 11 country offices and headquarters in Ottawa. There is currently no shared tool for estimating the true cost of a staff position — managers submit budget requests based on base salary alone, systematically omitting statutory employer contributions (8–28% depending on country), replacement costs, and leave liability. The result is recurring budget shortfalls, rework between HR and Finance, and delayed hiring decisions.

The NI Staff Cost Scenario Planner is a ready-to-deploy, browser-based tool that delivers band-accurate employment cost, replacement cost, and leave cost modelling across all NI locations — in under 60 seconds, with no spreadsheet required.

The Problem
  • No consistent method for estimating total employment cost across NI's 11 locations — managers use base salary only, leaving employer burden unbudgeted
  • Replacement costs (averaging 40–70% of annual salary) are routinely absent from position requests, surfacing as a Finance correction after approval
  • Leave cost modelling (maternity, medical, extended) is rebuilt manually by HR and Finance for each request, with no standard assumptions
  • NI's official salary bands — Canada Band 1–10 and Birches Group BG-05 to BG-13 — are not embedded in any planning tool; out-of-band offers go undetected until the offer stage
  • Cross-location comparisons require multi-day coordination between Country Offices, HR, and Finance before a single number is confirmed

Programs & Country Office Feedback — P&C FY2026 Planning Cycle

Programs is NI's largest employer of country-office staff. Country Directors and Program Managers must model hypothetical positions for donor proposals and budget submissions before engaging Finance — a process that today produces inconsistent estimates and creates rework for all three functions.

Use Case Scenarios
HiringManager · HR BP · Finance
New Position Budget Request

A program manager in Ethiopia selects BG-09 in the band picker. The tool instantly returns base salary (ETB + USD), CPF contributions, benefits loading, and total annual employment cost — ready to paste into the budget submission. No HR or Finance involvement required at this stage.

ReplacementHR BP · Country Director
Resignation Backfill Decision

A Country Director receives a resignation. The Replacement Cost tab models recruitment agency fees, advertising, interview hours, onboarding cost, and the full ramp-up productivity gap — giving Finance a defensible total before a backfill decision is tabled at EXCOM.

Leave PlanningHR BP · Finance BP
Maternity / Medical Leave Budget

HR models a 17-week maternity leave in Canada: gross pay, government benefit recovery (55%), net employer cost, and optional backfill daily rate. Finance receives a single agreed number for the mid-year forecast rather than a bespoke manual estimate.

Proposal CostingPrograms · Country Office
Donor Proposal Staff Budget

A Programs Officer building a USAID proposal for Senegal models three proposed positions using the official BG bands. The employer burden and benefits loading are automatically applied, ensuring the proposal budget aligns with NI's actual cost structure — reducing revision cycles with Finance.

Location CompareSr. Management · HR
Strategic Workforce Decision

A VP assessing where to locate a new regional role uses the Compare tab to view employment cost, replacement cost, and leave cost side-by-side across six country offices in a single view — a comparison that previously took two to three days of back-and-forth to produce.

Band ComplianceHR BP · Compensation
Offer Letter Review

Before extending an offer, HR enters the proposed salary for a Band 6 role in Ottawa. The compa-ratio and band position bar instantly flag whether the number is within range, at risk of compression, or outside band — without opening the Salary Admin Guidelines DOCX on SharePoint.
