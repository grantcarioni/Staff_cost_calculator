Staff Cost Scenario Planner
Business Case — Internal Tool Deployment  ·  FY 2025–26
Prepared by: Sr. Director, People & Culture
Date: May 2026
Status: For Review & Approval
Classification: Internal

Nutrition International operates across 11 country offices spanning three regions. Planning the true cost of hiring, replacing, or managing the leave of a staff member currently requires manual spreadsheet work, local HR interpretation, and Finance validation — a process that is inconsistent, time-consuming, and often completed too late to influence a hiring decision.

This business case recommends the immediate deployment of the NI Staff Cost Scenario Planner, a self-service web tool that gives managers and HR business partners real-time, band-accurate cost modelling for any position across all NI office locations — no spreadsheet required.

The Problem

Current State Gaps

  • No single, consistent tool exists for estimating total employment cost — managers submit budget requests with base salary only, missing employer contributions of 8–28% depending on country
  • Replacement costs are routinely underestimated or omitted from budget submissions, despite averaging 40–70% of annual salary for mid-level positions
  • Leave impact (maternity, medical, long-term) has no standard cost model; Finance and HR must rebuild estimates manually per request
  • Cross-location hiring comparisons require days of back-and-forth between HR, Finance, and Country Offices
  • NI's official salary bands (Canada: Band 1–10; Country Offices: Birches BG-05 to BG-13) are not embedded in any planning tool — managers enter salaries without knowing if they are within band

Programs & Country Office Feedback — P&C FY2026 Planning Cycle
The Programs unit is NI's largest employer of country-office staff. Country Directors and Program Managers routinely need to model hypothetical positions — new hires, backfills, and consultants — before approaching Finance. Without a shared tool, these requests arrive with inconsistent assumptions and create rework for both HR and Finance.
Use Cases

Hiring

Managers select a country and NI band level to instantly see the total employment cost — base salary + statutory contributions + benefits — before raising a position request. Supports accurate budget submissions.

Replacement

When a resignation is received, HR and Finance can model the full replacement cost — recruitment, onboarding, ramp-up productivity loss, and knowledge transfer — to inform the decision to backfill or restructure.

Leave Planning

For maternity, medical, or extended leave, the tool calculates gross cost to the employer including statutory offsets, backfill cost, and productivity impact — enabling timely budget adjustments.

Location Compare

Side-by-side comparison of up to all 11 NI office locations — employment cost, replacement cost, and leave cost — to support strategic workforce decisions and program proposal costing.

Band Compliance

Salary entries are validated against the official NI Canada Band 1–10 and Birches Group BG-05 to BG-13 structures. Green-circle and red-circle status is surfaced immediately, reducing out-of-band offers.

Proposal Costing

Programs staff can model staff costs for donor proposals — including employer burden, leave risk, and replacement assumptions — using the same assumptions Finance uses, reducing proposal revision cycles.

Benefits & Value
Time savings — HR, Finance & Programs
Eliminates 2–4 hours of manual cost modelling per hiring action. Estimated 120–320 hours reclaimed annually across all offices.
Improved budget accuracy
Employer burden rates (8–28% by country) and replacement costs are embedded in every estimate, reducing post-submission corrections.
Band compliance by design
Official NI salary structures are embedded directly in the tool. Out-of-band salaries are flagged before they reach an offer letter.
Empowers Programs teams
Country Directors and Program Managers can self-serve cost estimates, reducing dependency on HR and Finance for preliminary modelling.
Single source of truth
All 11 offices use the same statutory rates, FX assumptions, and band structures — eliminating version-control and inconsistency risks.
No implementation cost
Delivered as a standalone HTML file hosted on SharePoint or the HRIS portal. No software licences, infrastructure, or IT project required.
Recommendation
Deploy the NI Staff Cost Scenario Planner immediately to all HR Business Partners, Finance Business Partners, Country Directors, and Program Managers via the P&C SharePoint intranet. Establish a quarterly update cycle aligned to Birches Group data releases to keep salary structures current. Prioritize access for Programs unit staff ahead of the FY2026–27 proposal season.
